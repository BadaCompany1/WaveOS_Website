System Architecture

WaveOS is based on a deeply enhanced BSD operating system with a proprietary NeXT Kernel, programmed in Air, our own proprietary programming language. It also integrates Indigo Platform our own proprietary operating system. This combination offers a unique and highly customizable system architecture that sets WaveOS apart from the rest.
